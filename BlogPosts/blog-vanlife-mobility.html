Van-Life Mobility Routines

Sitting for long periods while living on the road can tighten up your hips, back, and shoulders. Here are 5 mobility drills that every van-life adventurer should add to their daily routine:

1. Cat-Cow Flow

Loosens the spine and improves posture after long drives.

2. Hip Flexor Stretch

Counteracts the tightness caused by extended sitting.

3. Shoulder Dislocates with Band

Improves shoulder flexibility and prevents stiffness.

4. Seated Forward Fold

Stretches the hamstrings and lower back.

5. T-Spine Rotations

Increases thoracic mobility, which is often restricted when driving or sitting.

Our Van-Life Fitness Guide includes a full set of mobility routines and easy workouts to do anywhere.
